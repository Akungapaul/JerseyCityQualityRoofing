- Case scenarios involve commercial properties, not residences

CRITICAL UNIQUENESS RULES:

- No two files should share the same opening sentence pattern
- The `extendedFaqs` must ask genuinely different questions per service (not just "How much does [X] cost in Jersey City?" for every service)
